Welcome to Legend of Ezera!

The game where you play while learning to code. You must defeat the 3 great dragons and gain their elemental stones before facing the Dragon King.

Your team consists of a warrior, a mage, and a ranger.

To begin, use createParty("warriorName", "mageName", "rangerName");

where "warriorName", etc. are the names you have chosen for your heroes. Then click 'apply' to run the code!
